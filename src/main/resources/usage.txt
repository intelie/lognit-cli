Usage:
  nit [QUERY] [OPTIONS]

Options:

  -s, --server <server>       logs into <server>
  -u, --user <user>           authenticates with <user>
  -p, --pass <password>       authenticates with <password>
  -n, --lines <number>        outputs last <number> lines (default: 100)
  -w, --wait <seconds>        timeout for history search (default: 10)
  -f, --follow                outputs new messages as they arrive (default: NO)
  -?, --help                  shows this help
